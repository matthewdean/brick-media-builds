LEGOLAND® California to Host Exclusive LEGO FriendsTM / Loose Chippin's Concert

New LEGO Friends Software To Feature British Pop Band

ENFIELD, CT - LEGO Media International --

The software division of the LEGO Group -- has teamed up with new all-girl British pop-band sensation Loose Chippin's and LEGOLAND California to launch LEGO Friends, a new CD-ROM game for girls ages 7 and up. To celebrate the September release of the groundbreaking LEGO Friends game, Loose Chippin's will make their North American debut with a series of open-air concerts at the Park. The band will perform at 10:30 a.m. Friday, September 24, and noon and 3 p.m. Saturday, September 25, at LEGOLAND in front of the LEGO Showplace theatre in the Imagination Zone block of the Park. The concerts are free to LEGOLAND guests.

"LEGO Friends is an aspirational game that will both challenge and inspire children to be creative with music and dance," explains Gregg Sauter, North American Director of Sales & Marketing, LEGO Media International. "By aligning LEGO Friends with an up and coming real pop band √ê Loose Chippin's - we're making the game even more relevant to girls."

During both days, park guests will have the opportunity to win prizes during the concerts, including free copies of LEGO Friends, LEGOLAND jackets and other prizes.
