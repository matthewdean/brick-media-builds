Park visitors will also be among the first people in North America to play the game inside the Maniac Challenge room, located in the Imagination Zone. The shops in LEGOLAND California will also be the first retail outlets to offer LEGO Friends for sale, and the game will be available for purchase in the park starting on September 24.

In a market dominated by products targeted at a male audience, LEGO Friends will provide girls ages 7 and up with their own world of creativity, fun and adventure where they join a girl band preparing for a big gig at school. As part of the agreement, Loose Chippin's will provide an exclusive music video based on their song "Inspirations" created solely for inclusion on the LEGO Friends CD - an industry first.

LEGO Friends includes many creative activities, such as composing songs, choreographing dance routines and preparing a stage show. All of these elements can be combined and altered, paving the way for the ultimate concert at the girls' school.

Running throughout the game are a series of animated narratives that introduce the player to the characters of LEGO Friends and the challenges they face.
