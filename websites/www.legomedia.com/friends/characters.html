THE BEST FRIEND: The main character has long hair with a fringe. She is likeable, and a good friend to the other girls. The words that describe her are: open, gentle, a peace maker and problem solver, always happy, tolerant, balanced, lively, outgoing, dynamic and energetic. She likes nature and has a pet dog called Disco. The bedroom location belongs to her and she plays the bass in Tuff Stuff.
ANITA: She composes the music for Tuff Stuff, and the band rehearses in her basement. She is a Eurasian girl with long straight black hair, and she is thin and tall (slightly lank in her demeanor). She has a strong personality: clever, witty, matter of fact and to the point (sometimes cutting), while at the same time remaining a sensitive and creative.
MIMI: She is a pretty black girl with her hair in small dreadlocks. She is slightly more developed physically than the others. She is a bit clumsy and tends to bump into things. She is very gentle and laughs a lot at funny situations or jokes, but sometimes the insecure side of her personality makes her seem indecisive. Mimi plays the electric guitar.
JOOLZ: She is a red head, and wears it short and spiky. She has a freckled face, and is a bit plump. She is shorter than the others. And Joolz is an extrovert; warm, funny and vibrant. She is joky (clownish) and likes to tease the others. Joolz and Anita sometimes do not get along as they tend to pick on each other when the situation allows for it. If anybody in the group is extreme, it would be Joolz. She plays the drums in Tuff Stuff.
EMMA: She is a petite blond, with long hair in a ponytail. She is thin but very agile (she does the dancing for Tuff Stuff). Emma is feminine and bubbly, though a bit vain. Her strengths are that she is a truly genuine person and a good friend. However, this sensitivity also makes it difficult for her to take criticism well.
DISCO: The best friend's dog, he is a knee high mongrel. He looks like a mix between a Spaniel and a fox terrier, with big floppy ears. His eyes and nose are very expressive and he communicates a lot with them. Disco is a crafty dog; he is most of the time pushing his luck by being disobedient and naughty. He enjoys spending his time with the girls and creating troubles for them He is certainly in bad need of training.
