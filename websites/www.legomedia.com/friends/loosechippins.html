Welcome to the Loose Chippin's web page! Wish you could be in a band too? Get on the road to pop stardom with LEGO Friends and prepare for the ultimate school gig!

LEGO Friends is a cool new CD-ROM for your PC that lets you join a girl band, just like we did a few years ago. You've got a big gig coming up at your school, so you better get the dance routines, music and stage show just right.

It's all set in Sunnyvale. There's loads of cool places to visit and fun things to do whether you're hanging out at Tina's Cafe, sleeping over at your friend's house or rehearsing in Anita's basement. There's even a diary for you to write down your secrets. The LEGO Friends in-game band is called Tuff Stuff and that's just what you need to be made of to succeed in the music biz. It's up to you to make sure your gig is a big success.

The Internet belongs to our generation and we have found a new way to get our message to you! Check back often for new music tracks to hear and download, live chats with the girls, and more.
