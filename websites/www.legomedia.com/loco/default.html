LEGO Loco let's you do virtually anything you can do with a real train set, er, virtually. It's an interactive desktop train set that's fully internet compatible.

The easy-to-use interface puts you in complete control of your own world... ideal practice if you want to grow up to be leader of one of the major superpowers. You can play with trains, and commuters and add sound effects and animations to bring your screen to life.

LEGO Loco is also a cool way to send messages to your friends. You can design your own postcards and deliver them on the mail train to other LEGO Loco users around the world.
