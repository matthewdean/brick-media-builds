Imagine you're playing real chess on your PC when, suddenly, your pieces come to life, do battle in 3D animation and turn a serious game into a fantastic story. No, you're not just dreaming, this is LEGO Chess.

We've based the game on mini-figures from two major LEGO SYSTEM TM themes - Western and Pirates - to help you get into this classic game of strategy. There's also a Crazy LEGO King character on hand to give you expert hints and tips.

You can view the 3D game from any angle and choose from seven different animated board backgrounds. So check it out, mate. (Check/mate. Geddit?)
