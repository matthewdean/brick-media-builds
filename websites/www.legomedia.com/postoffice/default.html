Why doesn't anyone send lovely friendly postcards any more? We say it's time to bring back the good old postcard - only this time in a state-of-the-art electronic form.

Here's what you do. Pick a friend, any friend. Click on one of these pictures. Prepare a short, friendly message (like 'Dear Sam, I recommend you eat five artichokes a day'). Then send it by e-mail.

P.S. Don't forget to tell your friends to visit our site, and be the first to tell them that all the games are available in the shops now!

P.P.S. If you like doing this, you'll love LEGO Loco which lets you design and deliver postcards by mail train to other LEGO Loco users around the world.
