At the time of writing, there are three LEGO Media International software titles in the marketplace, and six more in advanced
stages of production. Initially we are concentrating on high quality computer and console software for children aged between
two and twelve, produced in association with leading developers.

LEGO Media International is the publisher and takes overall marketing responsibility for all software ventures. We aim to
maintain synergy with other LEGO product ranges and bring distinctive LEGO values to this market, including creativity,
innovation and a sense of reward.

Our software range encompasses four strategic areas: LEGO Games, LEGO Constructive, LEGO Learning and LEGO For Girls.


LEGO Games

A range of exciting games based in LEGO environments, offering all the fun and unique sense of reward children associate with the LEGO brand. Developed predominantly for PC CD-ROM, several titles will also be available on Sony PlayStation and Nintendo 64. The first release in this category is LEGO Chess on PC CD ROM.


LEGO Constructive

Based on LEGO bricks and models, this is a unique category in the software area. It features non-competitive, single player interactive titles which challenge children to create and construct. Freed from practical constraints, children can give full rein to their creativity. There are currently two titles in this category LEGO LOCO and LEGO Creator.


LEGO For Girls

In a market dominated by products targeted at boys, these role-playing activity titles are developed to reflect the needs and interests of girls.


LEGO Learning

Entirely based on LEGO Duplo and designed for the benefit of pre-school children, these titles promote subliminal learning. While the accent is firmly on fun, they develop specific children's skills and abilities.
