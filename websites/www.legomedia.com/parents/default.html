Technology has made a huge impact on the playing patterns and expectations of children. The challenge for LEGO Media International is to fire their imagination in new ways, expanding their sense of discovery and challenging their powers of creativity.

Our background of 65 years in children's toys has provided us with a special insight into the way children think. We also take pride in our unique point of difference - excellence. All products are tested to ensure they help children develop self-confidence through play. (Results from initial testing of LEGO Creator, LEGO Loco and LEGO Chess show that children love them. Parents are equally impressed: LEGO Loco and LEGO Creator have just received Parents Information Network Gold Awards.)
