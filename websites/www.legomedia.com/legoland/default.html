Design, build and manage your very own LEGOLAND Park on your computer, using elements from LEGO SYSTEM themes: Western, Adventurers and Castle. 'Free Play Mode' allows you to build freely with access to a wide variety of attractions. 'Game Mode' poses a slightly different challenge in that your Park's success relies totally on the actions you take. Make your Park a success and you'll receive rewards and surprises, including special Park attractions and the famous Minilands.
