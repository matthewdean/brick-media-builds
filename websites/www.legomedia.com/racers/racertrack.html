Imperial Grand Prix
Ahh, there's nothing like a sandy beach, a cool breeze and a few palm trees to put you in the mood for some high-speed racing. That's the case with the Imperial Grand Prix, the first track you'll race in a LEGO Racers circuit race.
While the surroundings may be relaxing, the track itself has plenty of hazards and secrets. To start with, watch out for the spinning crate after the first sharp turn on the track. You'll need to avoid it by having a good sense of timing and driving to the left or right around it. Making contact with the crate will send you for a nasty spin!
Keep clear of the crate!
After you've made your way past the spinning crate, keep your eyes open for some wooden barrels hidden in the mines. When you find them, blast the barrels with a projectile power-up (the cannon ball works well here) to open up a secret passageway!
Blast these barrels to open a secret passage.
Shoot through the secret passage and across the bridge to one of the tightest turns on the track. You should definitely use a power slide to get through this corner at high speed.
It's power slide time, baby!
